Crystal Creator

What is it?

Crystal Creator is an interactive web application based off of Matthew Epler's YouTube series on Designing Generative Systems with p5.js (with an extra shout out to Stephan Max for more inspiration). It creates geometric "crystals" from a set of randomly generated parameters.

How to use it?

This page loads with a new set of crystals every time. Click the die button in the bottom-right-hand corner of the screen to generate a new sheet of crystals. If you see one you like, click to download or edit it. In edit mode, you can tweak it to your liking, or create a new one of your own!

Why?

Crystal Creator was initially created in 2020 to help combat shelter-in-place boredom and depression. It served as a creative outlet and marks the beginning of my discovery and exploration of generative art.
